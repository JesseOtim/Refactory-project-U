Our History

The open field, the livestock, the pasture and trees represent the vision of massaja division farm (ufarm), established in January 2023. Ufarm is an embodiment of freedom which is the amalgamation (uniting) of several farms in uganda under one umbrella. This idea was generated after immense research into the agricultural sector by a few individuals who discovered that a surprisingly huge number of farmers in the country or people who were involved in agriculture found it difficult to find reliable market for their products. Different products have different seasons and that had caused a lot of retreat from agriculture by many individuals. Then an idea spark in the middle of the storm, Massaja farm envisioned a society where each and every one gets their fair share of natures gift. Horticulture, Dairy, Produce and Poultry products all at your fingertips, at your disposal. An idea for an online platform was born to ease interaction between farmers and customers, products and their respective markets to enable easy acquisition of quality products for the consumers and income for the farmers.
